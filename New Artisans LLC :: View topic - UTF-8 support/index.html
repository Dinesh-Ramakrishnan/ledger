I may have missed something, but Ledger seems to lack UTF-8 support. The Unicode strings get through ungarbled, but when Ledger trims the string (such as in the register display), it sometimes ends the string on the first byte of a multibyte character, which yields question marks on the output. Multibyte characters also seem to increase the string length by more then one, which results in badly aligned fields in the output.
